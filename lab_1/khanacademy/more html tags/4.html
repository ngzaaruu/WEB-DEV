“Turtle” Cake

  • Time: 1hour 30 minutes
  • Serves: 10 portions
  1. Batter of Cake
  2. Cream of Cake

Batter of Cake

Ingredients Quantity
Wheat flour 2 cups
Eggs 5 pcs
Sugar 2.5 cups
Cocoa powder 4 tbsp
Sour cream (15%) 2-2.5 cups
Butter 100-120 g
Baking soda a pinch

Step 1: Crack the eggs into a bowl and add the sugar.

Step 2: Whisk 5 eggs with 1.5 cups of sugar and a pinch of baking soda until fluffy.

Step 3: Add 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder and mix well.

Step 4: Gradually add 2 cups of flour, stirring until smooth.

Step 5: This is your batter.

Step 6: Line a baking tray with parchment paper. Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F). Spoon the batter onto the parchment in small portions (about half a teaspoon each), forming small round cake layers.

Step 7: Bake for 7–10 minutes — they bake very quickly.

Cream of Cake

Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 1 cup
Softened butter 100 g
Cocoa powder 2 tbsp
Sour cream (15%) 2 cups

Step 1: Mix all ingredients with a mixer until smooth and creamy.

Step 2: Dip each baked cake piece into the cream.

Step 3: Stack them into a mound on a plate or serving tray, forming a “turtle shell” shape.

Step 4: If desired, drizzle the cake with chocolate glaze.
